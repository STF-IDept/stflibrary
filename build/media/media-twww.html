About Tangled WeBB We Weave

The verbosely-named Tanbled WeBB We Weave was another of the short-lived publications from the Golden Age of STF Media. Published by Canadian members Jim Armstrong and Mohammed Younis, TWWW lasted seven issues as "The Canadian Paper", longer than most of its competitors at the time.
